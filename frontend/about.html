Our Mission

We believe that no good food should go to waste while millions are hungry. Our platform bridges the gap between those with surplus and those in need.

Founded in 2026, FoodWasteRelief has grown into a community of thousands of donors, NGOs, and volunteers dedicated to an efficient, transparent, and technology-driven food distribution network.

Join the Movement

The Problem

Over 1/3 of all food produced is wasted globally, contributing to 8% of total CO2 emissions.

The Solution

By digitizing the donation process, we reduce the time from "surplus" to "served" by 60%.
